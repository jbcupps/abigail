# Abigail — Your Personal AI Family Companion

**The private coordinator that manages your family’s personal AI Entities.**

Abigail is the smart, private manager that lives in your home. You (the mentor or family head) create and customize individual **Entities** — each one a unique AI companion tailored to a person or purpose (one for you, one for your partner, one for the kids, etc.).

Your family talks directly to these Entities. Abigail coordinates everything behind the scenes: memory, skills, security, and growth.

### Why Families Love Abigail
- **Complete privacy** — everything stays on your computer. No accounts with big tech companies.
- **Real power when you want it** — connect any Entity to the strongest cloud models (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, xAI, etc.) with one click. You’re never locked to one company.
- **Grows with your family** — teach Entities new skills, memories, and preferences over time.
- **Simple for everyone** — your kids or parents just chat naturally; you control the advanced settings.

### Quick Start (5 minutes)
1. Install and open Abigail
2. Create your first Entity (give it a name and personality)
3. Let your family start chatting with their own Entity
4. Connect any Entity to powerful cloud models whenever you want more capability

No accounts. No data sharing. Just your family and the Entities you control.

### For the Curious: Where Abigail Came From
Abigail began as part of a larger research vision for trustworthy, decentralized AI (see the original paper: [Toward a Decentralized Trust Framework for Verifiable and Ethically Aligned AI](documents/Toward-a-Decentralized-Trust-Framework.pdf)).  
We took the best parts of that vision and made them simple, safe, and useful for real families.
